## About Owamni

**Restaurant:** Owamni by The Sioux Chef
**Chef:** Sean Sherman
**Location:** Minneapolis, Minnesota (overlooking Mississippi River)
**Awards:** James Beard Award Winner 2022
**Cuisine:** Indigenous cuisine featuring regional native ingredients

**Notable Features:**
- Bison ribeye (signature dish)
- Spoon cake dessert
- Mississippi River/St. Anthony Falls view
- Indigenous ingredients sourcing
- Wild teas and beverages
- Pre-contact Indigenous cuisine (no wheat, dairy, beef, pork, or chicken)
