## About Al's Breakfast

Al's Breakfast is a legendary 14-stool counter diner in Dinkytown, Minneapolis, operating since 1950. It's known for:
- The narrowest restaurant in Minneapolis (just 10 feet wide)
- 14 counter stools only - no tables
- Famous blueberry pancakes
- "Wally" - poached eggs over hash browns
- Weekend lines that wrap around the block
- James Beard semi-finalist recognition

## Image Usage Guidelines

These images are representative stock photos that capture the essence and style of Al's Breakfast. For the most authentic experience:
- Use images that emphasize the intimate, counter-only seating
- Highlight the classic diner aesthetic (red stools, chrome, narrow space)
- Show the food in a simple, unpretentious way
- Avoid overly polished or modern-looking imagery
