# The Borough - Image Sources

## Required Images

Download these 3 images and place them in this directory:

### 1. interior-pool-01.jpg
- **Source**: Google Maps photos for "The Borough" at 317 W Morgan St, Raleigh, NC
- **What to look for**: Interior shot showing pool tables and neon beer signs
- **Alternative**: Yelp user photos or Instagram @theboroughraleigh

### 2. neon-bar-01.jpg
- **Source**: Instagram @theboroughraleigh or Yelp
- **What to look for**: Bar area with colorful neon signs and tap handles
- **Alternative**: Google Maps user-submitted photos

### 3. exterior-01.jpg
- **Source**: Google Street View or Google Maps
- **What to look for**: Street-facing exterior of the dive bar
- **Alternative**: Yelp exterior photos

## Image Requirements
- Format: JPG
- Min width: 1200px
- Max file size: 500KB
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 4:3 preferred

## Download Tips
1. Right-click image and "Save Image As"
2. Rename to match filename above
3. Verify image is not copyrighted/watermarked
4. Prefer user-submitted photos over stock photos
