# Watts & Ward - Image Sources

## Required Images

Download these 3 images and place them in this directory:

### 1. interior-dark-01.jpg
- **Source**: Google Maps photos for "Watts & Ward" at 200 S Blount St, Raleigh, NC
- **What to look for**: Dark, intimate speakeasy interior with vintage decor and low lighting
- **Alternative**: Yelp photos or Instagram @wattsandward

### 2. cocktails-01.jpg
- **Source**: Instagram @wattsandward
- **What to look for**: Beautifully crafted cocktails with artisanal garnishes and quality glassware
- **Alternative**: Google Maps user photos, Yelp reviews with images

### 3. entrance-01.jpg
- **Source**: Instagram or Yelp
- **What to look for**: The hidden/underground entrance aesthetic
- **Alternative**: Google Maps if available, or another interior shot if entrance not photographed

## Image Requirements
- Format: JPG
- Min width: 1200px
- Max file size: 500KB
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 4:3 preferred

## Download Tips
1. This is an upscale speakeasy - look for professional-quality photos
2. Focus on the intimate, dark atmosphere
3. Cocktail close-ups should show craftsmanship
4. Avoid photos with too many people (privacy concerns)
