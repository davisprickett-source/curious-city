# Architect Bar - Image Sources

## Required Images

Download these 3 images and place them in this directory:

### 1. exterior-bungalow-01.jpg
- **Source**: Google Street View or Google Maps for 108 S Person St, Raleigh, NC
- **What to look for**: Charming converted bungalow exterior showing the residential character
- **Alternative**: Yelp exterior photos or Instagram @architectbarraleigh

### 2. cocktails-seasonal-01.jpg
- **Source**: Instagram @architectbarraleigh
- **What to look for**: Seasonal craft cocktails featuring local North Carolina spirits
- **Alternative**: Google Maps or Yelp review photos showing drinks
- **Note**: Look for cocktails with fresh ingredients, seasonal garnishes

### 3. interior-cozy-01.jpg
- **Source**: Google Maps or Instagram @architectbarraleigh
- **What to look for**: Intimate interior with warm wood showing house-party vibe
- **Alternative**: Yelp interior photos showing the cozy, residential feel

## Image Requirements
- Format: JPG
- Min width: 1200px
- Max file size: 500KB
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 4:3 preferred

## Download Tips
1. Focus on the "converted house" aesthetic - residential charm
2. Look for warm lighting and intimate spaces
3. Seasonal cocktails should look artisanal, not too fussy
4. Avoid overly crowded shots - this is about intimacy
