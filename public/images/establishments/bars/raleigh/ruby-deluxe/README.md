# Ruby Deluxe - Image Sources

## Required Images

Download these 3 images and place them in this directory:

### 1. drag-show-01.jpg
- **Source**: Facebook page "RubyDeluxeRaleigh" or Instagram @rubydeluxeraleigh
- **What to look for**: Drag performers on stage with colorful lighting
- **Alternative**: Google Maps reviews, fan photos from social media
- **IMPORTANT**: Get permission if using photos of specific performers, or use wide shots

### 2. interior-lights-01.jpg
- **Source**: Instagram @rubydeluxeraleigh or Google Maps
- **What to look for**: Interior with vibrant neon lights and welcoming LGBTQ+ atmosphere
- **Alternative**: Yelp photos showing the bar area and colorful lighting

### 3. dance-floor-01.jpg
- **Source**: Facebook or Instagram stories/posts
- **What to look for**: Dance floor with crowd energy (consider privacy - avoid clear faces)
- **Alternative**: DJ booth or another angle showing the late-night vibe

## Image Requirements
- Format: JPG
- Min width: 1200px
- Max file size: 500KB
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 4:3 preferred

## Important Notes
- **Privacy**: Avoid clear photos of patrons' faces without permission
- **Drag performers**: Credit performers if possible, or use wide shots
- **Representation**: Choose images that show the welcoming, inclusive atmosphere
- **Check social media for latest event photos**
