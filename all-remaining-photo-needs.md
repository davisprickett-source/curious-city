## Recommended Image Sources

### For Establishments (Bars, Restaurants, Coffee Shops)

1. **Official Websites**
   - Most establishments have press kits or image galleries
   - Look for "About" or "Press" pages

2. **Google Maps/Places**
   - Business photos often include official images
   - User-contributed photos can be high quality

3. **Yelp Business Pages**
   - Owner-uploaded photos are usually best quality
   - Look for "See all photos" and filter by "Business photos"

4. **Instagram**
   - Check `@[business_handle]` for official images
   - Request permission for use

5. **Facebook Business Pages**
   - Businesses often post promotional images
   - Check photo albums

### For Historical/Cultural Sites

1. **Wikimedia Commons**
   - Search for location name + "Creative Commons"
   - Verify license allows commercial use

2. **Library of Congress**
   - `loc.gov` for historical photos
   - Public domain materials

3. **State Historical Societies**
   - Minnesota Historical Society, Oregon Historical Society, etc.
   - May require permission or attribution

4. **Local Tourism Boards**
   - Meet Minneapolis, Visit Tampa Bay, Travel Portland, etc.
   - Often have press resources

5. **Flickr Creative Commons**
   - Search with license filter for commercial use
   - Requires attribution
