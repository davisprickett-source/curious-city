## Image Requirements per Establishment

### Standard Images (1-3 per establishment)
- Exterior shots showing signage/facade
- Interior atmosphere shots
- Signature items/drinks/food

### Menu Images (PRIORITY)
- Try to get embedded/downloadable menu images
- Photos of physical menus
- Menu boards
- Special menu items

## Image Sources to Check (in order of preference)

1. **Official Website** - Primary source
2. **Google Maps Photos** - Often high-quality user photos
3. **Instagram** (@handle from data)
4. **Yelp** - User photos
5. **Press/Media Coverage** - Articles about the establishment
6. **Facebook** - Business page photos
