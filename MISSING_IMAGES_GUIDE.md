## SEARCH STRATEGY TIPS

### Best Historical Photo Sources:
1. **Library of Congress** - Prints & Photographs Division
2. **Wikimedia Commons** - Public domain historical images
3. **State Historical Societies** - Each state has archives
4. **University Libraries** - Special collections often digitized
5. **Local Museum Archives** - City-specific museums
6. **NOAA/USGS** - Natural disasters, geological events
7. **National Archives** - Federal government events
8. **Flickr Commons** - Museums share public domain photos

### Image Requirements:
- **Format:** PNG or JPG
- **Size:** At least 1200px wide recommended
- **Licensing:** Public domain, Creative Commons, or images you have rights to
- **Quality:** Clear, relevant to the specific story (not generic stock)

### Filename Convention:
- Use lowercase
- Use hyphens for spaces
- Be descriptive but concise
- Example: `tesla-colorado-laboratory.png`, `1964-earthquake-damage.png`
